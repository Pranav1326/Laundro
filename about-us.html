Welcome to LaundroMINT
Your Trusted Laundry Partner

At LaundroMINT, we’re redefining the laundry experience, making it convenient, hygienic and affordable for students and institutions alike.
Founded in 2018, LaundroMINT started with a vision to provide students living in hostels and dormitories across India with a reliable, cost-effective and high-quality laundry service. As we expanded, we recognized that the demand went beyond just a student-friendly service — institutions also needed a solution that could help lower their operational costs. With this in mind, LaundroMINT offers a business-to-business solution, relieving institutions from managing their own laundry facilities or relying on unorganized local vendors who often lack quality standards.

Meet Our CEO

At the helm of LaundroMINT, Mr. Sameer Deodhar is the visionary leader who saw a pressing need and turned it into a solution that would change the landscape of laundry services for students. Recognizing that millions of students in India, particularly those in hostels, faced challenges in accessing affordable, hygienic and convenient laundry options. Sameer set out to provide a seamless on-premise laundry service that would cater to this growing market.

With over 10 million students living in hostels across India, Sameer understood that the need for a reliable and affordable laundry solution was immense, but he also saw that the problem extended beyond just students it was a challenge for educational institutions as well. Traditional laundry services often came with high overheads, logistical hurdles and inconsistent quality.
‍
Sameer’s solution was simple yet revolutionary: LaundroMINT would not only meet the needs of students, but also offer educational institutions a worry-free, cost-effective laundry service without requiring them to invest in laundry infrastructure or manage the ongoing expenses.
‍
By partnering with LaundroMINT, educational institutions get a trusted brand providing hygienic, fast and affordable laundry solutions all while focusing on their core mission of education. Institutions benefit from the convenience of having a professional laundry service on campus without the burden of setup, maintenance or operational costs. In return, LaundroMINT delivers consistent, high-quality laundry services that satisfy both students and management alike, creating a win-win situation for everyone involved.

Sameer’s leadership is grounded in a Customer-first Philosophy that emphasizes innovation, operational efficiency and a commitment to sustainability. Under his guidance, LaundroMINT continues to grow as the preferred laundry partner for educational institutions across India, helping to streamline operations while providing exceptional services for students.

Our Journey

From a single laundromat in 2018 to a trusted name in the industry, LaundroMINT’s growth has been nothing short of impressive. Our story reflects our commitment to innovation, quality and sustainability. Here’s a snapshot of our journey:

2018
The Launch

Our journey began in Pune, serving 600 hostel students with a simple goal: to offer a hassle-free laundry solution.

2019
Expanding Reach

With the success of our first location, we expanded to 4 service locations, providing laundry services to 950 students across multiple campuses. This expansion was driven by positive student's feedback and wide acceptance by institute management.

2022
Scaling Up

By 2022, we had expanded to 7 service locations across Pune, Mumbai, and Ahmedabad, serving over 2,000 students. Our offerings grew beyond basic laundry to include ironing and enhanced efficiency.

2023
Continued Growth

In 2023, LaundroMINT reached 10 service locations, serving 3,150 students. We continued expanding, further solidifying our presence across India.

2025
Expanding Horizon

Today, LaundroMINT operates across 16 service locations in 5 states Gujarat, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra and Telangana—serving over 6,000 students. Our growth is fueled by a strong commitment to quality, convenience and sustainability.

What We Do...

LaundroMINT provides on-premise, hygienic, fast and affordable laundry services to educational institutions, catering to students, hostels and dormitories. We partner directly with universities, colleges, schools and private hostel to provide a seamless & reliable laundry solution that helps institutions cut operational costs while improving student satisfaction.

For students, LaundroMINT means convenience of having on-premises laundry, offering more time for studies, social activities and personal growth.

Life Busy? Wash Easy! That’s our promise to students whether you’re juggling academics, extracurriculars or just trying to relax, we take care of your laundry so you can focus on what matters most.

For institutions, we eliminate the need for any upfront investment in laundry infrastructure, by offering a business-to-business solution that ensures high standards of hygiene and quality.

Why Choose LaundroMINT?

Hygienic & Safe: We use the best quality detergents and modern washing technologies to ensure that your clothes are always fresh, clean and hygienic.

Affordable & Convenient: Our services are designed to be budget-friendly for students and we offer flexible, convenient options that fit your busy lifestyle.

Fast & Reliable: We understand that students need efficiency, which is why we ensure quick turnaround for washing, drying and delivery every time.

B2B Partnerships: For educational institutions, we offer a professional laundry service without the burden of investment or management. We handle everything, while you provide a valued service to your students.

Sustainability: At LaundroMINT, we’re deeply committed to sustainability. Our operations are designed to minimize our environmental impact, from using eco-friendly detergents to optimizing water and energy usage. We save approximately 31 million liters of water across the areas that we serve.
