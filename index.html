Frequently Asked Questions

What does LaundroMINT do?

LaundroMINT provides trusted on-campus laundry solutions, offering hygienic, convenient & affordable services to hostels, colleges & universities across India.

What services does LaundroMINT provide?

We offer a complete end-to-end laundry solution, including washing, drying, ironing and folding, using smart, eco-friendly machines and technology-enabled service management.

How is LaundroMINT different from other laundry service providers?

• Zero investment is required from the institution.
• Separate wash cycle for each student to maintain the highest hygiene standards.
• Smart IoT-enabled machines with real-time tracking.
• Professional support team on-site.
• Focus on hygiene, speed and convenience for students and parents.

what type of machines & detergent does LaundroMINT use?

We use commercial-grade, water-efficient washing machines to ensure optimal fabric care and resource efficiency. For detergents, we use world class liquid detergent, which is skin-friendly and gentle on garments, ensuring both cleanliness and fabric safety.

Can LaundroMINT customize services based on the institution’s needs?

Absolutely. We offer tailored solutions to fit each campus's unique requirements, including service frequency, machine placement and additional laundry needs.

How does the LaundroMINT model work?

We operate on a zero-investment & business-to-business (B2B) model. Institutions pay a fixed monthly fee for fully managed laundry services, with no upfront costs for equipment, setup or maintenance.

Is LaundroMINT eco-friendly?

Yes! Sustainability is at the core of our operations. Our machines are designed to save water, energy and we promote environmentally responsible laundry practices on every campus we serve.

Is the laundry facility staffed or self-service?

Our laundromats are fully staffed by trained professionals, ensuring students enjoy a hassle-free, reliable and hygienic laundry experience without managing the process themselves.

How do students avail of LaundroMINT services?

Students can easily drop off their laundry at the campus laundromat and receive clean, ironed clothes within a 48-hour turnaround time. Some campuses may also offer app-based service tracking.

How can an institution partner with LaundroMINT?

It’s simple! Institutions can schedule a meeting with our team through the website. Once we understand your requirements, we tailor a customized proposal. Upon agreement, we conduct a site visit and swiftly set up a fully operational on-campus laundromat.
